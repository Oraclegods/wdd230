Ibadan Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Site Name

Ibadan Chamber of Commerce. The name represents the city where the chamber operates and aligns with the mission to support businesses in Ibadan.

Site Purpose

The Ibadan Chamber of Commerce website will serve as a platform to promote local businesses, attract investors, and provide valuable resources for business growth and networking.

Scenarios

  • What are the upcoming business networking events in Ibadan?
  • How can I join the chamber and what are the benefits?
  • Where can I find a directory of businesses associated with the chamber?

Color Schema

Primary: #004080 (Used for headers, navigation bar) /div>
Secondary: #f39c12 (Used for buttons, call-to-action elements)

Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat, sans-serif
  • Body Text: Open Sans, sans-serif
